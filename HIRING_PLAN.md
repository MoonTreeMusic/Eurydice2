## Hiring Rationale

We are building a music app. At this stage, execution speed matters more than headcount. The founding team is intentionally lean.

**CTO (hired)**: Owns all technical execution — architecture, code, infra, and delivery. This is a build role, not a strategy role.

---

## Next Hires (Priority Order)

### 1. CMO / Head of Growth (Q3 2026)
**Why**: Once an MVP exists, we need someone driving distribution. Music apps live or die on discovery and retention.  
**Trigger**: First working build shipped.

### 2. UX Designer (Q3 2026)
**Why**: Music listening is viscerally personal. Bad UX kills retention before marketing gets a chance.  
**Trigger**: First user testing session reveals friction points.

### 3. Second Engineer (Q4 2026)
**Why**: CTO should stay in architecture and key systems; a second engineer handles features and maintenance.  
**Trigger**: Backlog exceeds 4 weeks of CTO capacity.

---

## Roles We're NOT Hiring Yet

- Head of Finance: Too early; CEO owns budget
- QA Engineer: CTO ships with tests; revisit at scale
- Data Engineer: Revisit when user base exceeds 1k DAU

---

## Hiring Principles

1. Hire for execution, not credentials
2. Every hire must make the team faster, not slower
3. No leadership vacuums — define scope before hiring
4. Fire fast when it's not working
